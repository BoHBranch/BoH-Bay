Hestia’s Bastion - Basic Rules
Disclaimer: These rules, while enforced, may be subject to change at any
time for any reason. Just because it’s not in the rules doesn’t mean you
can’t get in trouble for it, use your brain and common sense to know what’s
right and wrong or ask an admin for clarification!
Rule #1: Role Playing
-
Participating in roleplay is encouraged, as we are a role playing server. The amount of
RP may vary situation-to-situation, but should remain a constant element.
Disregarding RP entirely is generally unacceptable. Wordlessly and emotelessly getting
into a fight is an example of disregarding RP. Antagonists, Command, and Security
have stricter regulations on this.
Not every fight is equal in stakes or circumstances. You don’t have to write a paragraph
about how you slide across the cafeteria to start a fistfight. In an antag situation, a
quick, short emote about pulling out your gun and firing works just fine, or you may
instead elect to use the aim function without any triggers and yell to get down. These
are just examples, and if you are uncertain, feel free to ahelp.
Rule #2: Character Backstory/Naming/Records
-
Your character's backstory should make sense within our lore, and as of now our lore is
changing and currently being worked on so we may push away from Bay’s- lore. In turn
your character can still have a story that is different from the lore as long as it does not
directly contradict lore in any way.
Character naming should always be taken seriously, as this is an RP server, and you
should attempt to follow the below when it comes to naming.
● NO: Naming a character based off something else (e.g. Peter Griffin, Michelle Obama,
Gordon Freeman, ect.) *This rule doesn't apply if you're the creator of said character
● NO: Naming a character that sounds vaguely similar or is in reference to another
character (e.g. Tonald Drump, Griffin Peter, Freedon Gorman, ect.)
● DO: Attempt to follow naming schemes of your species, unless your characters
backstory gives reason otherwise.
● NO: Naming characters in a way that signifies a lack of RP. (e.g. Chad Thundercock,
Orange Fluffytail, Killer Jack, ect.)
● NO: Adding titles to your characters name, (e.g. Doctor/Dct., Mr./Mrs., Jr./Sr., ect.)
*Using things like "Jack Moore IV" is acceptable as long as the number isn't absurdly
high.
● DO: Have a surname, regardless of species. The only exception to this is GAS, Robots
and Vat Growns. - In the case of Vat Grown, if you lack a last name, you must substitute
it with something such as digits, location name, etc.
Character records at this time are not a requirement on any character, however this
may change in the future for heads of staff. The general rule of thumb is the following:
1. Employment records should contain your qualifications, a doctor may be an expert in
medical but applied sciences very little, as such they shouldn't have qualifications to do
everything.
2. Medical records should contain postmortem instructions and general information on the
character (Name, DoB, height and weight ect.). Non-human synths are the exception to
needing medical records.
3. Security records should label anything you've done out of game purely for backstory, or
things you've done in game that you want to remain canonical. *Note: Having 53 counts
of murder on your records and being the warden doesn't look good, and your boss plus
Central Command may terminate your character from said position.
You have plenty of leeway for your characters creation-wise. Just use common sense.
Rule #3: Stay in Character
-
IC in IC. OOC in OOC.
IC stands for “In Character”, while OOC stands for “Out of Character”.
Being in character is vital to roleplay and game experience. Do not use emoticons and
text talk (e.g. “I c u”, “wtf man”, “how r u c:”).
Do not bring anything that is IC related into OOC chat, such as telling everyone in OOC
that you are dead, if there is an issue always ahelp rather than immediately jumping to
OOC.
Being out of character should only happen in the appropriate OOC channels on discord
and when using LOOC/OOC in game, this also means that you should never bring
anything OOC related into IC, such as your dislike of another player so you avoid
healing them when they come through medical, or giving them a more harsh sentence
in security. Conversely, bringing IC hate into OOC/LOOC rants is frowned upon and
likely to have you muted.
Rule #4: Erotic Role Play
-
*Disclaimer: While ERP is 100% allowed on this server, you are NOT protected by the
rules in a majority of situations. If you’re found IC, that’s it. You act it out.
There are a few minor exceptions to the above:
-
Intentionally targeting ERPers, for the sake of attacking ERPers. This is disallowed.
Conflict should happen naturally, not through “ecks dee they erp therefore I kill”.
-
Intentionally breaking into secluded areas with the intention to harass ERPers.
4.1: ERP should never stop you from playing your role. For example, if you are the only Doctor,
and you ERP neglecting your job’s duties. This may lead to a job ban, or, if excessive enough, a
server ban. Seriously, don’t waste slots just to get your rocks off.
Rule #5: Metagaming
-
Communication taking place to share IC information regarding an ongoing round OOCly
is a big no. This includes, but isn’t limited to:
● DMs/Voice Chat regarding ongoing in round happenings.
● Posting about the ongoing round OOCly.
Rule #6: Self Antagging
-
Self antagging is the process of carrying out incredibly hostile actions, or generally
antagonist-like behavior without reason. Unless you have some sort of massive,
absolute proper reason to be doing things such as sabotaging vital station components,
or venting areas, it’s self antagging. Use your brain.
As with other rules, if you’re unsure, please, do ahelp for clarification.
Rule #7: Powergaming
-
Powergaming is doing more than your job entails, such as being a doctor that knows
how to set up the engine, or being a cook that knows how to do surgery. Certain things
can be made an exception to this rule, but generally you shouldn’t be MacGyver.
Powergaming also includes using game mechanics to your advantage in either combat,
arrest, or roleplay when you shouldn’t be able to:
-
Buckling yourself to a chair mid combat to cancel countdowns on certain abilities.
-
Using instant grabs and pins on an applicable species to facilitate removing someone from the
round permanently without reason, or to avoid RP.
Rule #8: 18+
-
As stated elsewhere, you MUST be eighteen or older to play in this community. If you’re
discovered to be underage, or suspected of such, you’ll be immediately perma-banned until you
can provide evidence otherwise.
If you’re caught introducing, or harboring underage individuals, you’ll be subject to the same ban
as them. - As this is rather serious, those caught doing such will not be able to appeal under any
circumstance.
Rule #9: Meta Grudging
-
Meta Grudging is the act of using meta knowledge to hinder, interfere, or overall ruin another
player's experience because of past rounds or things that have happened in OOC. Seeing a
character you've never met come into medbay but refusing treatment just because you know the
player is meta grudge.
Understand that there is a difference between meta grudge and not liking another character.
Two characters who have interacted with each other numerous times may have some kind of
bad-blood, causing the two to act hostile, cold, or even scoff at one's presence with an
occasional insult. This is character development, and unless it hinders or ruins your game, it's
not meta. Always ask an admin for clarification if you feel you are a victim of such.
